== Description ==
Body mass index form can be added to your content areas with [cal-bmi] shortcode, basal metabolic rate form with [cal-bmr], waist-to-hip ratio form with [cal-whr], one-repetition maximum form with [cal-orm], Maximum Heart Rate form with [cal-mhr], exercise volume form with [cal-ev], practice load form with [cal-pl]. NOTE: Please install the Elementor plugin on your WordPress if you see a problem with the appearance of the form.
